How to Fold an Easy Origami Lion

Traditional Origami lion Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Folding time: N/A
  • Model designer: N/A
  • Paper: N/A

Step: 01

Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

Step: 02

Fold both sides to the centre along the dotted lines.

Step: 03

Fold a tiny bit of the top layers of paper behind along the dotted lines.

Step: 04

Fold the paper behind along the dotted lines.

Step: 05

Fold the paper in half.

Step: 06

Make an Inside Reverse Fold along the dotted line.

Step: 07

Open up the paper by folding along the dotted line.

Step: 08

Fold the top of the paper down along the dotted line.

Step: 09

Fold the bottom of the top layer of paper underneath along the dotted line.

Step: 10

Fold the right side of the paper over along the dotted line.

Step: 11

Fold the paper down along the dotted line.

Step: 12

Fold the paper over again along the dotted line.

Step: 13

Make a tiny Inside Reverse Fold at the end of the tail.
